ABOUT

We are proud to present to you DEEP TROPICS 2017, Nashville’s first ever electronic music, art and style festival!

DEEP TROPICS sets sail in the heart of Music City on August 5-6, 2017 at the historic War Memorial Auditorium & Courtyard. DEEP TROPICS will host 2,500 guests per day (Ages 21+) . . . two days with two eye-catching stages featuring performances by forward-thinking artists, musicians, muralists, and tastemakers from all over the world.

Staying true to theme, DEEP TROPICS will transform the city landscape into an oasis by creating a sustainable eco-system of lush, tropical bliss.

Discover a deeper sense of self, a deeper connection with community and a deeper experience through music and art.

Sustainability

Deep Tropics wove sustainability into every layer of preparation and planning. With a vision and commitment, we are strengthening community, supporting local economy, educating patrons, conserving energy and water, minimizing waste and providing healthy, organic food and beverage options. Collaboration with our sponsors, vendors, volunteers and forward-thinking partners has made Deep Tropics goal of sustainability possible.

F.A.Q.

BASICS

Rain or shine. No refunds, exchanges or cancellation. Everyone and all items are subject to search upon entry and at any time within the site. Line-up and set times are subject to change. All patrons are subject to Deep Tropics Purchase Policy.

PETS

There are no pets allowed.

AGE POLICY

Please note that Deep Tropics is 21+ Admission Only with valid government issued Photo ID checked at the gate. No valid government issued photo ID = No Entrance.

RE-ENTRY

General Admission 2-day pass holders will be allowed to exit and enter the festival a maximum of once (1) per day. VIP pass holders have unlimited re-entry.

FESTIVAL TIMES

VIP Pool Party from 12pm to 2PM
Main Event Gates open at 2PM
Outdoor Stage ends at 10:30PM // Indoor Auditorium Stage ends at 12:30AM

WATER STATION

There will be a water station on-site at the Main Event. Water will also be sold.

ATMS

There will be ATMs on-site at the Main Event.

LOST & FOUND

There will be a Lost & Found tent on-site at the Main Event. If you have lost an item and can’t make it to the tent, please contact us via the CONNECT page.

GET INVOLVED

Getting involved is simple! Head over to the CONNECT page and send us an email. We have provided pre-written subject lines to make it easier for you.

TICKET OPTIONS

PRE-PARTY & LATE NIGHT TICKETS
