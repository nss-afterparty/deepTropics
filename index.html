ABOUT

Deep Tropics is a two-day music, arts and lifestyle festival that launches August 5th & 6th, 2017. The War Memorial Auditorium & Courtyard in Nashville, TN will host 2,500 people per day (Ages 21 and up) with two stages of music. The programming includes forward thinking artists, musicians, muralists and tastemakers from all over the world. Deep Tropic’s mission is to create an experiential brand that re-connects our community with authentic culture.

True to theme, we are transforming the traditional venue landscape into a lush tropical ecosystem and adult playground. Deep Tropics will facilitate organic food and beverages in addition to generating a sustainable festival infrastructure. Discover a deeper sense of self, a deeper connection with community and a deeper experience through music and art.
